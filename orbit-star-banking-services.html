What is Corporate Banking Assistance?

A specialized suite of services that connect UAE businesses to local and international banks. With increasingly strict global compliance (KYC and AML) policies, getting an account approved requires precise documentation and strategy.

Documents Typically Required

  • Valid Trade License / Incorporation Certificate
  • Memorandum of Association (MOA)
  • Passports & Emirates IDs of shareholders
  • Visa copies of shareholders
  • Proof of physical address (Ejari or DEWA bill)
  • Corporate Business Plan or Company Profile
  • 6 months personal bank statement of shareholders
