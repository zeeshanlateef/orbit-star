Comprehensive Business Setup in Dubai Mainland

Setting up a business in Dubai Mainland offers unparalleled flexibility and access to the local and international markets. Orbit Star Services is your trusted partner to navigate the legal and administrative complexities, allowing you to focus on growing your business while we handle the setup seamlessly and efficiently.

Why Choose Dubai Mainland for Business Setup?

Strategic Location

Dubai's strategic position bridges East and West, serving as a dynamic hub for international trade, commerce, and innovation.

100% Ownership

Foreign investors can now maintain 100% ownership of their mainland companies across a wide range of commercial activities.

No Currency Restrictions

Benefit from the ability to freely repatriate capital and profits outside the UAE with no restrictions on fund transfers.

Flexible Office Locations

Mainland companies have the flexibility to rent office space anywhere in Dubai, allowing you to choose the most strategic location.

Step-by-Step Process for Business Setup in Dubai Mainland

01
Initial Consultation

Discuss your business ideas, activities, and goals with our experts to determine the most appropriate legal structure and jurisdiction for your specific needs.

02
Trade Name Registration

We help you select and register a compliant and unique trade name with the Department of Economy and Tourism (DET).

03
Company Incorporation

Drafting the Memorandum of Association (MOA) and Local Service Agent (LSA) agreement, ensuring all legal documentation is accurate and legally binding.

04
Documentation and Approvals

Compiling all necessary paperwork and securing initial approvals from relevant government departments and regulatory authorities.

05
Office Space and Ejari

Securing your commercial office space and obtaining the Ejari (tenancy contract), a mandatory requirement for issuing your business license.

06
Bank Account Setup

Assisting you in gathering documents, liaising with bank managers, and opening your corporate bank account swiftly.

07
Ongoing Support

Post-setup assistance including visa processing, PRO services, and continuous administrative support to keep your operations running smoothly.
