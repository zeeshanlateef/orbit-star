Renew your business license smoothly without operational downtime. Manage tenancy contracts, EJARI, and government approvals effortlessly.

What Is Trade License Renewal?

Every business operating in the UAE must renew its trade license annually to maintain legal status. Delaying renewal can result in severe fines, suspension of company bank accounts, and visa cancellations. The process involves renewing lease contracts, coordinating with the DED or Freezone authorities, and securing external department approvals. Navigating the renewal criteria can be complex—our experts handle it all for you.

Who Needs Renewal Services?
  • Mainland DED licensed entities
  • Freezone companies across zones
  • Firms requiring updated EJARI records
  • Businesses needing municipal permits
Why Professional Handling Matters

  • Accurate tenancy/office lease updates
